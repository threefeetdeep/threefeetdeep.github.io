about me

My name is Oli Bailey, an electronics engineer living and working in the south of England. Since 2018-2019 or so, I’ve become interested in machine learning, data science and statistics, so I set up this site to document my learning process. I also post other random stuff here too…

I’m also very interested as a lay “armchair scientist spectator” in the latest theories on the origin of life on earth (i.e. the first replicating structures, the RNA-World hypothesis, protocells, self-assembly of primitive cellular molecules, etc.), as well as a general interest in pop-sci reading.

I named this website after this terrible joke:

“Did you hear about the statistician that drowned crossing a river?"

“It was three feet deep on average."

You can contact me by this email: threefeetdeep at outlook dot com. Most of my code and notebooks are available from my github account.
